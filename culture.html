Culture of Karwar

Discover the rich cultural heritage, traditions, and local lifestyle of Karwar.

Traditional Arts

Karwar has a rich tradition of folk arts including Yakshagana, a traditional theater form, and various folk dances that reflect the local culture and traditions.

Cuisine

The local cuisine is dominated by seafood dishes prepared with coconut and spices. Don't miss trying the famous fish curry, neer dosa, and other coastal delicacies.
