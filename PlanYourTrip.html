Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Karwar is from October to March, when the weather is perfect for outdoor activities and beach visits.

Enjoy clear skies, cool temperatures, and great beach experiences!

How to Reach Karwar

By Road: Well-connected highways with regular bus services from major cities.
By Rail: Karwar Railway Station provides easy train access across Karnataka.
By Air: The nearest airport is Dabolim Airport, Goa, about 100 km away.
